10. Veda Online#

10.1. Veda-TIMES / SATIM Attributes#

Attribute

Description

Purpose in Model

ACT_BND

Activity bound for a process, indicating limits on activity.

Used to set upper/lower bounds on process activity.

ACT_COST

Cost associated with the activity of a process.

Represents operating costs of a process.

ACT_CUM

Cumulative activity over a period.

Tracks cumulative energy demand or supply over time.

ACT_EFF

Efficiency of a process in converting energy.

Used to model process efficiency, affecting energy input-output.

ACT_UPS

Activity of a process in a specific time slice.

Tracks activity in a defined time period (e.g., seasonal or daily).

CAP_BND

Capacity bound for a process.

Sets the maximum or minimum installed capacity for technologies.

COM_AGG

Commodity aggregation for energy flows.

Aggregates energy flows across multiple processes or technologies.

COM_BNDNET

Net commodity bounds for supply or demand.

Limits the net flow of energy or materials in/out of processes.

COM_CSTNET

Net cost of a commodity.

Tracks the net cost of commodity flows between processes.

COM_CUMNET

Cumulative net flows for a commodity.

Tracks total cumulative commodity flows across the system.

COM_FR

Commodity flow ratio between processes.

Specifies ratios for how commodities flow between processes.

COM_PEAK

Peak commodity demand or supply.

Sets a maximum limit on peak commodity flow.

COM_PKRSV

Peak reserve for a commodity.

Specifies reserve margins for peak commodity supply.

COM_PROJ

Commodity projections over time.

Projects future commodity supply or demand trends.

FLO_EMIS

Emissions related to process energy flows.

Tracks pollutant emissions based on energy flows.

FLO_MARK

Flow markers for energy or material transfers.

Sets upper and lower bounds on energy/material flows between processes.

FLO_SHAR

Share of flow that a process must supply.

Specifies the percentage share of energy or material flow.

G_DRATE

Discount rate applied to future costs/benefits.

Used to discount future costs in economic calculations.

G_DYEAR

Base year for discounting.

Specifies the base year for discount rate calculations.

G_YRFR

Year fraction for discounting.

Fraction of a year used in discount rate calculations.

IRE_FLO

Flow of imported/exported energy.

Tracks international energy imports/exports.

IRE_PRICE

Price of imported/exported energy.

Specifies the price for energy imports/exports.

NCAP_AF

Availability factor for installed capacity.

Indicates the availability of installed capacity for use.

NCAP_AFA

Annual availability factor.

Specifies the yearly availability of a process.

NCAP_AFC

Capacity factor by season or time slice.

Adjusts availability by specific time periods.

NCAP_AFX

External availability factor.

Availability based on external conditions (e.g., weather).

NCAP_BND

Installed capacity bounds.

Sets upper/lower limits on installed capacity.

NCAP_CHPR

Charge rate for storage technologies.

Specifies the charging rate for energy storage technologies.

NCAP_COST

Cost of installed capacity.

Represents capital cost for installing new capacity.

NCAP_DISC

Discharge rate for storage technologies.

Specifies the discharge rate for storage technologies.

NCAP_ELIFE

Economic lifetime of installed capacity.

Determines how long a technology can be used before replacement.

NCAP_FOM

Fixed operation and maintenance cost.

Fixed cost for operating and maintaining installed capacity.

NCAP_ICOM

Investment cost for new capacity.

Tracks the initial investment cost for new technologies.

NCAP_ILED

Lead time for new capacity installations.

Specifies time needed to install new capacity.

NCAP_PASTI

Past investment in installed capacity.

Tracks previous investments in infrastructure.

NCAP_PKCNT

Peak contribution of installed capacity.

Specifies the contribution of capacity during peak demand.

NCAP_START

Start-up cost for installed capacity.

Tracks costs associated with starting up new capacity.

NCAP_TLIFE

Technical lifetime of installed capacity.

Specifies the duration before technical degradation or replacement.

PRC_ACTFLO

Activity flows for processes.

Tracks energy/material flow for specific processes.

PRC_CAPACT

Capacity-activity ratio for processes.

Specifies the ratio between process capacity and activity level.

PRC_REFIT

Refitting costs for existing technologies.

Tracks costs for upgrading or refitting existing technologies.

PRC_RESID

Residual capacity of processes.

Tracks remaining usable capacity for processes over time.

RCAP_BND

Residual capacity bounds.

Sets upper/lower bounds on residual capacity.

RPT_OPT

Option reporting for optimization.

Specifies which options to report during optimization.

SHAPE

Shape or time profile for energy demand/supply.

Defines the temporal profile for energy use across time slices.

STG_EFF

Storage efficiency for energy storage technologies.

Specifies efficiency of energy storage processes.

UC_ACT

Unit cost for process activities.

Tracks the cost per unit of activity for processes.

UC_CAP

Unit cost of installed capacity.

Tracks the cost per unit of installed capacity.

UC_COMNET

Unit cost of commodity flows.

Tracks the cost per unit of commodity flows in the system.

UC_COMPRD

Unit cost of commodity production.

Tracks the cost per unit for producing energy/materials.

UC_IRE

Unit cost for energy imports/exports.

Specifies the unit cost for cross-border energy flows.

UC_NCAP

Unit cost of new capacity installations.

Tracks the cost for each unit of new capacity installed.

UC_RHSRT

Unit cost for short-term reserve.

Tracks the cost for short-term energy reserves.

UC_RHSRTS

Unit cost for seasonal reserves.

Tracks the cost for maintaining seasonal reserves.

VDA_CEH

Carbon emissions for specific processes.

Tracks carbon emissions from energy processes.

VDA_EMCB

Emission control costs and benefits.

Tracks costs and benefits associated with emission controls.

10.2. User Constraints#

User Constraint

Description

Purpose in Model

U_CCS_CAPT

Carbon capture and storage (CCS) capacity constraint.

Limits the maximum capacity of CCS technologies in the system.

U_CCS_PWR

Power output constraint for CCS processes.

Limits the power output from processes using CCS technology.

U_CCS_PWRC

Constraint on power generation using CCS.

Restricts power generation using CCS to a certain capacity or output.

U_CCS_PWRG

Growth constraint for power generation using CCS.

Sets growth limits on power generation using CCS.

U_CCS_STOR

Storage constraint for CCS.

Limits the storage capacity for captured carbon.

U_NH3CTL

Ammonia control technology constraint.

Places restrictions on ammonia control technologies in energy generation or industry.

UC_Growth_Batteries

Growth constraint for battery technologies.

Sets a limit on the annual growth of battery storage capacity.

UC_Growth_CO2EQS

Growth constraint on CO2 equivalents.

Limits the growth of CO2-equivalent emissions from energy processes.

UC_Growth_CO2S

Growth constraint on CO2 sequestration.

Sets a limit on the growth of carbon sequestration technologies.

UC_Growth_CSP

Growth constraint for concentrated solar power (CSP).

Limits the annual growth of installed capacity for CSP technologies.

UC_Growth_DAC

Growth constraint for direct air capture (DAC).

Restricts the expansion of DAC technologies used for carbon capture.

UC_Growth_ICPSCLE-E

Growth constraint for industrial coal processes with CCS.

Sets growth limits on the use of industrial coal processes with CCS in the electricity sector.

UC_Growth_IFBSCOA-E

Growth constraint for industrial biomass coal processes.

Limits growth in industrial biomass coal processes for electricity generation.

UC_Growth_IMISCOA-E

Growth constraint for industrial miscellaneous coal processes.

Restricts growth in the use of miscellaneous coal processes in the electricity sector.

UC_Growth_INFSCOA-E

Growth constraint for industrial steel coal processes.

Limits the expansion of industrial steel production using coal in electricity generation.

UC_Growth_IOTSCOA-E

Growth constraint for industrial oil processes.

Restricts growth in industrial oil-based processes used in electricity generation.

UC_Growth_IOTSELC-E

Growth constraint for industrial electricity processes.

Limits growth in electricity production by industrial processes.

UC_Growth_IOTSELC-N

Growth constraint for industrial electricity processes (non-renewable).

Limits growth in non-renewable industrial electricity generation processes.

UC_Growth_IOTSGAS-E

Growth constraint for industrial gas processes.

Sets limits on the growth of industrial gas processes in electricity generation.

UC_Growth_IOTSOHF-E

Growth constraint for industrial oil heating processes.

Restricts growth in the use of industrial oil for heating in the electricity sector.

UC_Growth_Nuclear

Growth constraint for nuclear technologies.

Limits the expansion of nuclear power generation capacity.

UC_Growth_PV

Growth constraint for photovoltaic (PV) solar technologies.

Limits the annual growth of PV solar capacity in the system.

UC_Growth_TFHCV1ELC-N

Growth constraint for first-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in non-renewable electric vehicles of class 1.

UC_Growth_TFHCV2ELC-N

Growth constraint for second-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Limits the growth of non-renewable second-class electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV2HGN-N

Growth constraint for second-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Limits growth in the use of non-renewable hydrogen vehicles of class 2.

UC_Growth_TFHCV3ELC-N

Growth constraint for third-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of third-class non-renewable electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV3HGN-N

Growth constraint for third-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Sets limits on the growth of third-class non-renewable hydrogen vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV4ELC-N

Growth constraint for fourth-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Limits the growth of fourth-class non-renewable electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV4HGN-N

Growth constraint for fourth-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of non-renewable hydrogen vehicles of class 4.

UC_Growth_TFHCV5ELC-N

Growth constraint for fifth-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Limits the expansion of non-renewable fifth-class electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV5HGN-N

Growth constraint for fifth-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of non-renewable hydrogen vehicles of class 5.

UC_Growth_TFHCV6ELC-N

Growth constraint for sixth-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Limits the annual growth of non-renewable sixth-class electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV6HGN-N

Growth constraint for sixth-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of sixth-class non-renewable hydrogen vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV7ELC-N

Growth constraint for seventh-class electric vehicles (non-renewable).

Sets growth limits on seventh-class non-renewable electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFHCV7HGN-N

Growth constraint for seventh-class hydrogen vehicles (non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of non-renewable hydrogen vehicles of class 7.

UC_Growth_TFLCVELC-N

Growth constraint for light commercial vehicles (electric, non-renewable).

Limits the growth of non-renewable light commercial electric vehicles.

UC_Growth_TFLCVOGSH-N

Growth constraint for light commercial oil and gas (non-renewable).

Restricts the growth of non-renewable oil and gas-powered light commercial vehicles.

UC_Growth_TPPRCARELC-N

Growth constraint for private passenger cars (electric, non-renewable).

Limits the annual growth of private passenger electric vehicles (non-renewable).

UC_Growth_TPPRCARHGNF-N

Growth constraint for private passenger cars (hydrogen, non-renewable).

Restricts the growth of non-renewable hydrogen passenger cars.

UC_Growth_TPPRCAROGSH-N

Growth constraint for private passenger cars (oil and gas, non-renewable).

Sets a limit on the growth of non-renewable oil and gas passenger cars.

Growth constraint for sport utility vehicles (electric, non-renewable).

Limits the expansion of non-renewable electric SUVs.

UC_Growth_TPPRSUVHGNF-N

Growth constraint for sport utility vehicles (hydrogen, non-renewable).

Restricts growth in the use of non-renewable hydrogen SUVs.

Growth constraint for public transportation buses (electric, non-renewable).

UC_Growth_Wind

Growth constraint for wind technologies.

UC_Growth_XICPELC

Growth constraint for imported electricity.

UC_Growth_XIFBELC

Growth constraint for imported fossil-based electricity.

UC_Growth_XIMIELC

Growth constraint for imported mixed electricity.

UC_Growth_XINDELC

Growth constraint for industrial electricity.

UC_Growth_XINDHGN

UC_Growth_XTRAELC

Growth constraint for electricity transmission.

Activity constraint for CCS in the industrial sector.

UCACT_CCSPower

Activity constraint for CCS in the power sector.

Sets a limit on the activity of CCS in power generation.

UCACT_IPP_BIO

Activity constraint for independent power producers using biomass.

Restricts the activity of biomass-based independent power producers.

UCACT_IPP_PULP

Activity constraint for independent power producers using pulp and paper waste.

Limits the activity of pulp and paper-based power producers.

UCACT_IPP_RECMAX

Activity constraint for renewable energy capacity.

UCACT_SteelScrap

Activity constraint for steel production using scrap metal.

UCACT_WIND

UCCAP_BATTR

UCCAP_DUMETR

UCCAP_WIND

Capacity constraint for wind energy technologies.
